The Oregon Department of Education had a grant of $62 million to match any school bond that passed in the state. School districts were prioritized by poverty, application order, and then the rest of schools were put on a waiting list.

Of the nine poverty prioritized schools, five bonds passed — matching $16.9 million and leaving $45.1 million left in the grant.
