The Gift That Gives Back

Receive yearly payments for life by making a gift to OPB. Earn a tax deduction and increase your income through a charitable gift annuity. Call us at 503.293.1941 or see the details below to learn more.

Step One

Make A Gift

If you or the recipient of the yearly payments are 65-years-old or older, you can make a gift of $10,000 or more in cash or stock to OPB.

Step Two

Receive Yearly Payments

OPB pays you yearly payments for the rest of your life. The amount of the payment is based on a competitive fixed rate of return. The older you are the higher the initial rate of return.

Step Three

Earn a Tax Deduction

A charitable gift annuity also makes you eligible for a charitable income tax deduction and possible additional tax benefits.

Step Four

Support OPB

The remainder of your gift will become part of your legacy supporting OPB's mission to serve our community.
